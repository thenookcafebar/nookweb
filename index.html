About Us

Welcome to The Nook Cafe-Bar – a cosy, vintage-inspired mobile café lovingly run by Helen & Chloe, a friend duo with a shared passion for homemade baking and heartfelt hospitality. At The Nook, everything we serve is made from scratch using quality ingredients, time-honoured recipes, and a good pinch of love. From our irresistibly gooey chocolate brownies to our golden oat flapjacks and indulgent cakes, each treat is baked in small batches to ensure freshness and flavour in every bite. Whether we’re popping up at your local market, serving at a special event, or delivering a custom order, our mission is simple: to spread a little joy through handmade sweetness and warm, welcoming service. Come say hello, grab a slice, and make yourself at home – wherever The Nook parks up!
